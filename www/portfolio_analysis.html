ZSM Opportunity Index for the ZSM Seven

This analysis evaluates each ticker in the ZSM Seven Model Portfolio based on current price relative to the 50-day moving average (MA50) and Relative Strength Index (RSI). The opportunity score ranges from -10 to 10, where positive scores indicate better buying opportunities, and negative scores suggest caution.

Scores are derived as follows:

  • High positive: Low RSI (<30) and price significantly below MA50.
  • Neutral (0): RSI around 50 and price very close to MA50.
  • High negative: High RSI (>70) and price significantly above MA50.
